Reading the Matrix

Each number = minimum d20 roll required (before modifiers).

Lower numbers mean a much higher chance to succeed.

Example:

A Purple Belt needs a 9 or higher on d20 to pass/sweep another Purple Belt (~60% success).

A Black Belt needs 10 to succeed against another Black Belt (50% success).

A White Belt needs 18 vs a Black Belt (15% success).
