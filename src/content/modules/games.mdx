# Games Hub

Interactive training games to improve spatial reasoning, memory, and pattern recognition.

## Available Games

### Centroid
Estimate the center of mass of scattered points. This game improves spatial reasoning and pattern recognition skills essential for understanding positioning and movement in grappling.

**How to Play:**
- Click on the grid to place your guess for the centroid
- Double-click or press VALIDATE to confirm
- Lower scores are better - aim for 0 points per round
- Complete 10 rounds with increasing difficulty

**Ideal For:**
- Developing spatial awareness
- Understanding center of mass concepts
- Quick decision-making under time pressure

### JJJ Memory
Match pairs of technique cards to enhance visual memory and technique recognition.

**How to Play:**
- Click cards to flip and reveal techniques
- Match pairs to remove them from the board
- Complete all pairs in the fewest moves and shortest time

**Ideal For:**
- Visual memory training
- Technique recognition
- Pattern matching
