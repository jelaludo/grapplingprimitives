# Flash Cards

Study and review techniques with interactive flash cards. Search, filter, and stage concepts for focused study sessions.

## Features

### Search & Filter
- **Search**: Type to find concepts by name or description
- **Category Filter**: Click category badges to filter by concept category
- **Sort Options**: Sort alphabetically or by category

### Study Modes

#### Grid View
Browse all matching concepts in a responsive grid. Click cards to flip and reveal descriptions.

#### Carousel View
Focus on one concept at a time with smooth navigation. Swipe or use arrows to move between cards.

#### Study Mode
Dedicated study session with:
- One card at a time
- Confidence rating (1-10 scale)
- Progress tracking
- Automatic progression

### Staging
Click "Stage" on any card to add it to your study list. Review staged concepts separately or use them for focused study sessions.

## Tips

- Use category filters to focus on specific areas
- Stage concepts you want to review later
- Rate your confidence in Study Mode to track progress
- Search is case-insensitive and searches all text fields
